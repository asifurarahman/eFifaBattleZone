## ğŸ“œ Match Rules

### Group Stage

* Format: BO1
* Match Length: **5-minute halves**
* Draws allowed
* Points System: Win = 3, Draw = 1, Loss = 0

### Double Elimination

* Format: BO1 for all matches including Grand Final
* Grand Final: Single decisive match (BO1) - Winner takes all
* No advantage rules - Pure single elimination match

### Tie-breakers (Group Stage) - FIFA Rules (FC 25 Compatible)

1. Points in all group matches
2. Goal Difference (GD) in all group matches
3. Goals For (GF) in all group matches
4. Points obtained in matches between tied teams (Head-to-Head)
5. Goal difference in matches between tied teams (H2H GD)
6. Goals scored in matches between tied teams (H2H GF)
7. Fair Play Points (FC 25 cards only: Yellow = 1pt, Red = 4pts - lower is better)
8. Drawing of lots (random selection)
